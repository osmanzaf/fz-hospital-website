FAQs

Common Questions

Answers to the most common questions from expecting mothers in Islamabad.

What is the cost of normal delivery at FZ Hospital? +
Delivery charges at FZ International Hospital depend on room type (private or shared), length of stay, and any additional procedures. WhatsApp us at +92 311 1529680 for a detailed, transparent quote. We offer competitive pricing with all-inclusive packages covering labor room, delivery, postnatal ward, and newborn care.
Do you handle high-risk pregnancies? +
Yes. Our FCPS gynecologists are experienced in managing all types of high-risk pregnancies including gestational diabetes, pre-eclampsia, placenta previa, multiple pregnancies (twins), IUGR, previous C-sections, and recurrent miscarriages. We have 24/7 emergency support and coordinate with our internal medicine and pediatric specialists as needed.
Is there a 24/7 emergency maternity service? +
Yes — absolutely. FZ International Hospital provides round-the-clock maternity emergency services. Whether you're experiencing labor pains, decreased fetal movement, bleeding, or any pregnancy complication at any hour of the day or night, our team is always ready. Call 051-4493031 or WhatsApp +92 311 1529680 immediately.
Can I choose between normal delivery and C-section? +
Your delivery plan is discussed with your gynecologist during antenatal visits. We always aim for safe normal delivery when possible. C-section is recommended when medically indicated for the safety of mother or baby. Dr. Tasmia and Dr. Sobia will guide you based on your specific case, medical history, and baby's position.
What antenatal care services are available? +
We offer complete antenatal care including all trimester consultations, blood tests, urine analysis, blood pressure monitoring, anomaly scan (18–20 weeks), growth scans, Doppler ultrasound, gestational diabetes screening, Group B Strep testing, and birth plan preparation. Same-day ultrasound reports are available at our in-house diagnostic unit.
Where is the hospital and how do I register my pregnancy? +
We are at 204 Street, Shaheen Commercial, Jinnah Garden, Islamabad — easily accessible from DHA, Bahria Town, PWD, Anchorage, Soan Gardens, and Rawalpindi. To register your pregnancy, simply WhatsApp us at +92 311 1529680 and we'll book your first antenatal appointment. Early registration (first trimester) is recommended for best care.
